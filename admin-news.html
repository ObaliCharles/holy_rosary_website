Holy Rosary Primary School Gulu serves families across Gulu City and the wider Acholi sub-region. We follow the Ugandan curriculum for P.1-P.7 and combine strong academics with faith formation, culture, and life skills. Our campus is a safe, caring place where every child can grow.
