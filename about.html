About Us

Welcome to Holy Rosary Primary School Gulu

Located in Laroo Division, Holy Rosary Primary School Gulu serves learners from Gulu City and surrounding districts in Acholi and Lango. We offer P.1-P.7 under the Ugandan National Curriculum with a strong focus on literacy, numeracy, science, and values. Learning blends classroom teaching with music, dance, drama, and agriculture that reflect Northern Uganda's culture. We work closely with parents and the parish to support every child's growth.

To provide inclusive, high-quality primary education that builds literacy, numeracy, character, and life skills for children in Northern Uganda.
