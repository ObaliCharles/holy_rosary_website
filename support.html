Parent Support

We walk with families all year

Holy Rosary Primary School Gulu partners with parents and guardians to keep learners safe, confident, and ready to succeed. Below you will find key information, FAQs, and guidance for every term.

Parent Support

Clear communication and shared responsibility help every child thrive.

  • Termly parent meetings and class WhatsApp groups for updates, reminders, and homework tips.

  • Daily communication through class teachers, the school office, and follow-up calls when needed.

  • Volunteer opportunities during reading days, chapel events, and community celebrations.

Admissions FAQs

Quick answers for new and transferring families.

Which classes do you admit? We enroll learners in P.1-P.7, with transfers accepted when space is available.

What documents are required? A recent report card, birth certificate or baptism card, and two passport photos.

Is the school day or boarding? Holy Rosary is a day school serving families across Gulu and nearby districts.

When will we hear back? Our admissions team responds within 2 working days.

Term Calendar

Typical term structure for the school year.

  • Term 1 (February-April): Orientation, baseline assessments, and mid-term break in March.
  • Term 2 (May-August): Continuous assessment, sports day, and mid-term break in July.
  • Term 3 (September-December): Revision, end-of-year exams, and graduation celebrations.

Exact dates are shared by circular at the start of each year.

School Policies

Our policies create a safe and focused learning environment.

  • Attendance and punctuality are monitored daily.
  • Uniform is worn Monday-Friday and during school events.
  • Homework is given weekly and checked by class teachers.
  • Positive discipline with no corporal punishment.
  • Mobile phones are kept by the office during school hours.
Safeguarding

Every child has the right to feel safe, respected, and protected.

Report any concern to the Headteacher, Senior Woman Teacher, or class teacher immediately.
